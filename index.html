Iphone Wifi connection

An example of this is Apple’s iphone. The first iphone was released on the 29th of June 2007. The original model could ring, send and receive text messages, send and receive email messages, browse the internet with a wifi connection and users could download custom applications. One of the flaws of the original model was the camera didn’t have a flash and the internet wasn’t fast enough when users didn’t have access to a wifi connection.
